🏛️ Custom Library

If you are a paid subscriber, in our private Library you will find everything I have ever published: articles, book reviews, and podcast interviews, easily browsable and filterable by topic 👇

Articles are embedded inside the platform, so you can read and watch everything without going anywhere 👇

The Library lives inside our community, which means you get a unified search experience that fetches content from both conversations and newsletter articles 👇

Search is perfect for when you are looking for keywords and people, but sometimes you have questions — not keywords. In these cases, you can chat with the Refactoring AI agent! 🤖

Our agent lives in two places:

  • Search — when you search for anything, the agent option will pop up as an alternative (see above).

  • DMs — you can write to the agent just like you would do to a person.

For any question you may have, the agent will reply with an answer that 1) is complete and workable by itself, and 2) contains references from articles and threads it draws from.
