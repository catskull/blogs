What is PostHog?

This newsletter is written by the team at PostHog, so you may be wondering: what is PostHog?

We’re all-in-one product and data platform that combines the following tools:

  • Product analytics – funnel analysis, retention, user paths, SQL queries, etc.

  • Web analytics – a simple overview of your visitors, pageviews, and sources

  • Session replay – observe real users, real usage in your web or mobile apps

  • Feature flags – feature management, instant rollbacks, local evaluation

  • Experiments– multivariate tests, automated statistical significance, JSON payloads

  • Surveys – NPS, free text, and multiple choice surveys

  • Error tracking – capture, group, assign, and solve errors happening in your app

  • LLM observability - see traces, costs, latency, and more usage of your LLM-powered features

  • Customer data platform – data pipelines to send data to webhooks, marketing destinations, and more

  • Data warehouse – join data from PostHog with external sources like Stripe or Postgres

Best of all, all this is free to use. We’re 100% self-serve, have generous free tiers for each product, and our pricing is transparent.
